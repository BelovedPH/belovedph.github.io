Frequently Asked Questions

What makes crochet bouquet flowers unique compared to real flowers?

Crochet bouquet flowers offer a timeless and enduring beauty. They are handmade with meticulous attention to detail, allowing for a level of customization not found in real flowers. Additionally, crochet flowers can be cherished as a lasting keepsake, bringing a touch of handmade artistry to any space.

Can I request specific flower types or colors for a custom order?

Absolutely! I love personalizing my creations. You can request specific flower types, colors, or even a combination of different blooms to match your preferences or a special theme. Customization adds a personal touch, making each bouquet a unique expression of your style.

How long does it take to complete a custom crochet bouquet order?

The time to complete a custom order can vary based on the complexity and size of the bouquet. Generally, I strive to complete orders in a timely manner while ensuring the highest quality. I'll provide you with a more accurate timeframe when discussing the details of your custom order.

Are crochet bouquet flowers suitable for special occasions like weddings or events?

Absolutely! Crochet bouquet flowers are a wonderful choice for special occasions. They add a distinctive, handmade charm to weddings, events, or any celebration. Plus, they serve as lasting mementos, allowing you to preserve the memories of your special day in a unique and beautiful way.

How should I care for crochet bouquet flowers to ensure their longevity?

Crochet bouquet flowers are low-maintenance but benefit from gentle care. Keep them away from direct sunlight and moisture. If needed, dust them lightly with a soft brush. With proper care, your crochet bouquet will stay vibrant and beautiful for years to come.

Do you ship crochet bouquet flowers internationally?

No, currently, we only offer shipping within the Philippines. We hope to expand our shipping options in the future.
